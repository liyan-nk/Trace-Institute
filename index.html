About Us

Welcome to Trace Institutes

Trace is a leading educational institution dedicated to shaping young minds and nurturing academic excellence. With our branches in Thirurkad and Mankada,
we have been serving the students of Malappuram, for the last 8 years.

At Trace we believe that every student has the potential to achieve greatness. Our mission is to provide a supportive and inspiring learning environment that enables students to unlock their full academic potential. We are committed to offering high-quality education and personalized guidance to students in High School and Higher Secondary

Available Classes

Trace for SSLC

We provide comprehensive and specialized classes for secondary school students in the SSLC level, offering a structured curriculum, expert guidance, and effective exam preparation strategies to help them achieve excellent results in their Exams

Junior Trace

Our tuition center offers comprehensive classes for 8th and 9th grade students, providing them with a strong foundation in key subjects and supporting their academic progress during their crucial high school years.

Trace Plus

We offer comprehensive higher secondary classes with a focus on entrance-oriented coaching, preparing students for their final examinations and various entrance exams.
